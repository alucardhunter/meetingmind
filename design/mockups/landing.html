Frequently asked questions

How accurate is the commitment extraction?

We use GPT-4o for extraction, which achieves ~90% accuracy on commitment identification. Combined with regex fallback for dates and dollar amounts, you should review and correct any extracted commitments — they're meant to save you time, not replace your attention entirely.

What audio formats are supported?

MP3, WAV, M4A, MP4, WebM, and OGG. Files up to 25MB (approximately 30 minutes of audio). For longer recordings, split them first or contact us about enterprise options.

Can I record directly in the browser?

Yes. MeetingMind uses the MediaRecorder API to capture audio directly from your microphone. Chrome, Firefox, and Edge are fully supported. Safari support is partial — we recommend uploading a recording instead.

What happens to my data?

Your audio and transcripts are encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not use your meeting data to train any AI models. You can delete your account and all data at any time — permanently.
